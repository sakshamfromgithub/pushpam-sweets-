Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Table

Ramu Sweets was born from a simple belief — that food made with love nourishes not just the body, but the soul. We started as a humble sweet shop and grew into a full restaurant, but our heart remains the same.

Located in Baraut Handia, Prayagraj, we serve our community with authentic Indian flavors, seasonal sweets, and a warm hospitality that keeps families coming back, generation after generation.
